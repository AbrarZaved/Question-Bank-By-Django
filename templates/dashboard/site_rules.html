Site Rules - DIU Question Bank

  • No Plagiarism: Ensure all submitted content is your own or properly cited.
  • Use Proper Categories: Post questions in the appropriate categories for better organization.

You must maintain a minimum ratio of 0.5 to avoid restrictions.

The ratio represents the relationship between the amount of content uploaded and downloaded. DIU Question Bank mandates a minimum share ratio of 0.50 or 50%. This means if someone downloads 10 Questions, they must contribute at least 5 Questions of relevant material to maintain a healthy ratio and avoid restrictions.

  • Contribute Your Resources: Share your question papers. By doing so, you help others and improve your upload data.
  • Engage Actively: Participate in discussions and answer questions. Your contributions will be valued and recognized.
